Our Solutions

Once you contact us, a customer service representative will be contacting you in the next 3 to 5 business day to set up an appointment. This first appointment will be free and will be the opportunity to further explain.

Package 1

Package 1: This is our most affordable package as there is no design/formulation input from the customer (if customer chose a pre-formulated product) or done by our team (if customer provides formulation).

  • No Design/formulation
  • Only 1 prototype for free
  • Manufacturing plan could change as needed
  • Warehouse space for shipping limited

Package 2

Package 2: Our bestseller package, more complete and budget friendly, allows customer to work in a comfortable position and be guided by our experts in every step:

  • Design/formulation process up to 3 meetings with customer service *
  • 3 Prototypes for free *1
  • Manufacturing plan crafted specifically for customer
  • Warehouse space reserved for customer up to a certain limit

* After meeting limit is reached. Each customer service meeting will be charged for an extra cost. Meeting time is up to 2 hours for packages 1 and 2 and 4 hours for package 3. *1 After limit each prototype paid separately.

Package 3

Package 3: Our most complete package, the no-worries package; we will take care of everything and let you decide on the important things. Customer will receive a complete report on all the big and small details of each process

  • Design/formulation process up to 8 meetings with customer service *
  • 5 prototypes for free *
  • Manufacturing plan crafted specifically for customer with optional scale up program for free
  • Warehouse space reserved with small limitations and no time fees

* After meeting limit is reached. Each customer service meeting will be charged for an extra cost. Meeting time is up to 2 hours for packages 1 and 2 and 4 hours for package 3. *1 After limit each prototype paid separately.

Why is not price reflected in the packages? Because each product is unique and its manufacturing process may be complicated, it is really hard to price on a flat rate. Our team is dedicated to provide the best service at the best price possible. Rest assured that we will do our best to fulfill all your requirements and answer all your inquiries.
